Why Choose Us

The AS Group Advantage

Strict Sourcing Control

Our process starts right at the roots with rigorous quality assessments and direct farm selection in Solapur—the heart of premium G9 banana cultivation.

Custom Packaging Architecture

We pack explicitly for transit durability using 20kg carets, specialized northern hand cuts (Punja), or heavy-duty 13kg export boxes.

Pre-Cooling Infrastructure

To prevent transit spoilage, long-haul configurations utilize heavy refrigerated fleets and 20-ton containers with built-in pre-cooling systems.

Multi-Channel Scale

We run direct active corridors serving local corporate clients, massive domestic wholesale mandis, and global port terminals simultaneously.

Quality Certified Delivery

Rigid tracking controls paired with robust handling standards to keep the natural ripening cycle perfectly paused until delivery.

End-to-End Logistics

From nimble 2-ton field pick-ups up to 20-ton global trade containers, our supply operations never leave the cold chain network.
